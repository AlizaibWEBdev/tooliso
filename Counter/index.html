About Word Counter Tool

Tooliso's Word Counter is a free online tool that instantly counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs in your text. It's perfect for writers, students, SEO specialists, and anyone who needs to analyze their text content.

How to use this word counter?

  1. Type or paste your text into the text area above
  2. The tool will automatically count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs
  3. Use the "Clear" button to reset the counter
  4. Use the "Copy" button to copy your text to clipboard

Who needs a word counter?

  • Writers & Bloggers: Check article length and word count requirements
  • Students: Meet essay or assignment length requirements
  • SEO Specialists: Optimize meta descriptions and content length
  • Social Media Managers: Stay within character limits for posts
  • Translators: Estimate project size and pricing
